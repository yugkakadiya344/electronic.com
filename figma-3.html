Smart TV


A television, commonly referred to as a TV, is an electronic device used for receiving and displaying moving visual images and sounds. It has been a staple of modern entertainment since its inception in the early 20th century and has evolved significantly over the years. Televisions primarily function by receiving broadcast signals, either over-the-air (OTA), through a cable or satellite service, or via the internet.
